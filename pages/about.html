Our Origin

The Art of the Stitch

Forged in the heart of London, refined in the tanneries of Tuscany. Oxen is a dialogue between traditional saddlery and modern architectural design.

Our Journey The Collection
The Beginning

A Single Workbench

Oxen didn't start in a factory. It began on a single oak workbench in a small London garage with a set of inherited saddlery tools and a refusal to accept the "fast-fashion" status quo.

Our founder sought to rediscover the tactile satisfaction of an item built by hand—something that wouldn't just last a season, but would survive a lifetime of travel and stories. Today, that same workbench sits at the center of our studio as a reminder of our roots.

The Build

The Ritual of Construction

01

Manual Grain Assessment

Every square inch of the hide is inspected by hand for strength and aesthetic character. We only cut from the most premium sections.

  • Thorough tactile check for structural weaknesses.
  • Rigorous evaluation of grain pattern alignment.
  • Exacting hand-cut templates to minimize waste.
02

The Twin-Needle Saddle Stitch

Using two needles and one continuous strand of waxed linen thread, we create a locking stitch that machine-sewing simply cannot replicate.

  • Precision placement of over 100 manual stitches.
  • Use of premium French waxed linen threads for resilience.
  • Self-locking tension knots to ensure eternal durability.
03

Beeswax Edge Burnish

Edges are sealed with organic beeswax and friction-polished until they achieve a glass-like finish that protects the hide forever.

  • Application of our proprietary organic beeswax blend.
  • Intense canvas friction to melt wax deep into fibers.
  • Burnished to a perfectly smooth, weather-resistant finish.
